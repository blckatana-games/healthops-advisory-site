Roy Nzurike

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Roy Nzurike is the Founder and CEO of HealthOps Advisory LLC, a healthcare operations consulting firm that helps organizations improve operational efficiency, optimize performance, and deliver high-quality patient care. With more than 15 years of experience in healthcare administration, Roy has led operational initiatives across behavioral health and correctional healthcare.

He partners with hospitals, behavioral health providers, correctional healthcare organizations, physician practices, and other healthcare entities to develop practical, data-driven solutions that enhance operational performance, improve patient and staff experiences, and support sustainable growth. Through HealthOps Advisory LLC, Roy is committed to helping organizations achieve operational excellence by delivering strategic, measurable, and lasting improvements.
